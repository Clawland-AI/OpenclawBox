## Validation Stages

### EVT (Engineering Validation Test) — May 2026
- 10 hand-built prototypes
- Verify all hardware components work together
- Test touchscreen integration and display quality
- Software boot and basic functionality validation
- Used for Kickstarter campaign demo photos/video

### DVT (Design Validation Test) — June 2026
- 50 units from production tooling
- Verify injection-molded enclosure fit and finish
- Thermal testing under sustained AI workload
- Drop testing and durability checks
- Final software image validation

### PVT (Production Validation Test) — July 2026
- 200 units from production line
- Full production process validation
- Yield rate optimization (target: >98%)
- Packaging and unboxing verification
- Units used for influencer seeding and QA

### Mass Production — July/August 2026
- 1000-2000 units (based on campaign results)
- Continuous QC sampling
- Final firmware flash and personalization
- Packaging and palletization

## Risk Buffer

All dates include a 2-4 week buffer. If we encounter delays, backers will be notified immediately with revised timelines via Kickstarter updates.
